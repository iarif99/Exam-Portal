⚡ VERB
🎯 PASSIVE
↔ CONTRAST
🎯 PRECISION
TOOL 1 — VERB TRANSPLANT
Weak verb খুঁজে strong verb বসাও। Synonym বদলালেই upgrade হয় না — power আসে PRECISION থেকে।
❌ reduce → decrease   ✅ reduce → curtail
FORMAT: WEAK → STRONG — বাংলা মানে
TOOL 2 — KILL PASSIVE, ACTIVATE SUBJECT
"It is..." / "There is..." ভেঙে দাও। Subject-কে active করো।
❌ It is important to take action.
✅ Policymakers must act decisively.
FORMAT: ❌ weak → ✅ strong
TOOL 3 — NOT A BUT B CONTRAST
Weak framing vs Strong framing দিয়ে impact বাড়াও।
FORMULA: "[Subject] must be treated not as [weak], but as [strong]."
❌ Renewable energy is important.
✅ Renewable energy must be treated not as a peripheral programme, but as indispensable national infrastructure.
FORMAT: topic — তোমার sentence
TOOL 4 — ONE PRECISION WORD RULE
প্রতি sentence-এ ঠিক একটা advanced word — বাকি সব simple।
❌ The ramifications necessitate fortification of macroeconomic resilience.
✅ Bangladesh must fortify itself against the next geopolitical shock.
FORMAT: precision word — example sentence
